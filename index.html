Member of SM Entertainment 's boy group NCT and subgroups NCT U and NCT 127 . They debuted in February 2018 with NCT U's 〈BOSS〉. In October of the same year, he joined NCT 127 from the album 〈Regular〉. In 2015, when he was a private trainee, he appeared on the Rookies Show with the Rookies members. On January 31, 2018, he joined NCT at the age of 19.

He is the lead vocalist and lead dancer in the team.
